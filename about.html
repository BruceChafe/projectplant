About Project Plant

Welcome to Project Plant, your friendly companion in finding the perfect leafy friend!

Project Plant was born out of our love for greenery and the desire to match people with their ideal plants. We believe that everyone deserves a plant buddy to brighten up their living space and bring a touch of nature indoors.

Our journey with Project Plant started at Get Coding, where we learned the art of web development. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, we embarked on the mission to create a web app that would help you discover your leafy soulmate.

We are passionate about plants and technology, and we've combined the two to create a fun and interactive quiz that will lead you to the plant that suits your lifestyle and preferences. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or a newbie, Project Plant is here to guide you on your plant journey.

So, are you ready to find your perfect plant companion? Take our light-hearted quiz and let the plant adventure begin!
